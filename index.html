Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Dots & Boxes is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Local Storage Data: Your name, country selection, volume preferences, and game settings are stored locally on your device
  • Game Statistics: Win/loss records and gameplay preferences are maintained locally
  • Room Codes: Temporary 4-letter codes for online multiplayer sessions
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we process is used exclusively for:

  • Personalizing your gaming experience with saved preferences
  • Enabling multiplayer functionality through temporary room codes
  • Maintaining game settings across browser sessions
  • Providing audio/visual customization options

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Local Storage Only: All personal data remains on your device
  • No Server Transmission: Your settings and preferences never leave your browser
  • Temporary Sessions: Online game rooms are ephemeral and automatically deleted
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies
  • Secure Connections: All online communications use encrypted protocols

4. Third-Party Services

Dots & Boxes operates independently without third-party integrations:

  • No advertising networks or trackers
  • No social media integrations
  • No external analytics services
  • No cloud storage or backup services

5. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • No age verification or personal information collection
  • Safe, family-friendly gaming environment
  • No chat or communication features with strangers
  • Compliant with COPPA and international children's privacy laws

6. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data through browser developer tools
  • Modify: Change settings and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Clear all data by clearing browser storage
  • Export: No data export needed as everything stays local

7. Data Retention

Data retention is entirely under your control:

  • Settings persist until you clear browser data
  • Game rooms are automatically deleted after inactivity
  • No long-term data storage on external servers
  • You can reset all preferences through the settings menu

8. International Users

Dots & Boxes respects global privacy standards:

  • GDPR compliant for European users
  • CCPA compliant for California residents
  • No cross-border data transfers
  • Local data processing only

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

  • Review our open-source code for transparency
  • Check browser developer tools to see local storage
  • All data processing happens locally on your device

Remember: You can clear all game data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this website.
